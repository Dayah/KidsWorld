WEDNESDAY, NOVEMBER 26
     I would like to direct your attention toward the bottom of Anti-KidsWorld's E-mails of harassment page. I noticed an e-mail supposedly from Lema (KidsWorld founder) to Anti-KidsWorld, but on closer inspection, I found that it is nothing but a fraud. Below is the header of the e-mail. From the header, you can tell where the e-mail originated.
From lema@kidsworld.org Tue Nov 25 14:23:10 1997
Received: from LKELLEY by exchange.nhboston.com with SMTP (Microsoft Exchange Internet Mail Service Version 5.0.1457.7) id W6G70LMX; Tue, 25 Nov 1997 17:23:58 -0500
From:     <Lema@kidsworld.org>
To:       <antikidsworld@hotmail.com>
X-Priority: 1
Subject:  YOU ALL SUCK
Mime-Version: 1.0
Content-Type: text/plain; charset="us-ascii"
Content-Transfer-Encoding: 7bit
     There are a few extremely strange things about this header. First, the message appears to have been sent to a HotMail account but was certainly not received from a HotMail account. We know this because the second line clearly tells us that someone with the login LKELLEY, sent the message through their mail server, exchange.nhboston.com. Note that themrbon's last name is Kelley. Second, the mail header is incomplete. Fields such as the e-mail client and originating IP are suspiciously missing. It is my conclusion from this evidence that themrbon has mailed this to himself and simply changed parts of the header to make it look as if it was sent by Lema.
