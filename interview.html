INTERVIEW WITH LEMA

     I managed to get an exclusive interview with Lema, founder of KidsWorld. Below you will find a transcript of exactly what was said. This is the one and only interview Lema has allowed.

Q: Do you keep up with the news or visit any of the KW vs. AKW pages or web boards?
A: Yes, [your] news cracks me up. I enjoy that, as far as the rest, I don't bother.
Q: Can you tell everyone the real reasons why LuceNT and themrbon were G-Lined?
A: LuceNT was G-Lined because he pulled a nutty and nuked a lot of the kids on the net. He knows that's a DoS attack and is not only against the law but [also] against KidsWorld's rules.
Themrbon was G-Lined because he was passing an application around trying to get a lot of the kids at KidsWorld to join a club he started, US Club. Joining a club would have been fine, but asking young boys the size of their penises and what kind of underwear they wear is very unacceptable. Some of the kids joined thinking he was in a position of authority on KidsWorld because he worked for KidsWorld previously but had to quit for a job.
Q: People have called KidsWorld “scared” because they did not post a rebuttal. Any comments?

Q: Themrbon has accused you of being prejudice against other preferences. Is this true?

Q: Has Anti-KidsWorld had any effect on the KidsWorld user base?
A: No, user base has been growing progressively right along.
Q: What do you think of Anti-KidsWorld's teamwork and cooperation?
A: Tough question, I really don't pay attention. In the beginning, I did to see if they had any real comments that we could correct, but they never did. I do know that they had asked The_Joker to come here and use their nicks, and that they and Bat have been here more than once, so I guess they have no club cooperation. As far as teamwork, it seems that themrbon is the speaker, but he continually says nothing. The others mimic or say the same things repeatedly.
Q: Is there any chance KidsWorld will sit down and talk with Anti-KidsWorld about this?
A: No, I tried to speak to themrbon in the very beginning. He twisted what was said. It'd be a waste of time.
